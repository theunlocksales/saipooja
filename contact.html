Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?
We accept orders from as low as 1,000 pieces for retail customers. For wholesale and bulk orders, we offer better pricing starting from 10,000 pieces. There's no maximum limit!
What is the delivery time?
Standard delivery takes 3-7 business days across India. For urgent orders, we offer express delivery options. Local orders can be dispatched the same day.
Do you provide custom printing?
Yes! We offer custom logo printing and branding on paper cups, plates, and other products. Minimum order quantity applies for custom printing. Contact us for details.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers, UPI, cash on delivery (for local orders), and credit facilities for regular customers. GST invoices provided for all transactions.
Do you deliver across all states in India?
Absolutely! We have a Pan India delivery network covering all 20 states. We ensure safe packaging and timely delivery to every corner of India.
Can I get product samples before ordering?
Yes, we provide free samples for bulk order inquiries. Contact us with your requirements and we'll arrange sample delivery.
