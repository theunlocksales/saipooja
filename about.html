Our Story

Building India's Most Trusted Disposable Brand

Sai Pooja Pattal Dona Bhandar was founded in 2010 with a simple vision - to provide high-quality, eco-friendly disposable products to businesses across India. What started as a small manufacturing unit in Dewas Naka, Madhya Pradesh, has grown into one of India's leading suppliers of premium disposable paper products.

Over the past 15 years, we have built an unshakeable reputation for quality, reliability, and customer service. Today, we proudly serve thousands of businesses across all 20 states of India - from small tea stalls to large event management companies, from hotels to corporate offices.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is equipped with modern machinery and follows strict quality control protocols. We are Gumasta Registered and maintain the highest standards of hygiene and safety. Every product that leaves our facility is thoroughly tested and inspected to ensure it meets international quality benchmarks.

What sets us apart is our commitment to our customers. We understand that businesses need reliable partners who can deliver on time, every time.
